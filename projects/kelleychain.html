  • Problem Statement

    Implemented a simple hyperledger network to understand and implement core Blockchain principles and concepts

  • Description

    A simple ledger implemented in Python while developing an understanding of Blockchain principles and practices, and how they can be applied within a business environment. The motivation was to become adept at Blockchain and distributed ledger systems, the important concepts and key use cases of Blockchain and how assets can be transferred in a distributed hyperledger network.
