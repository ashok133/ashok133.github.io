Digital Water Quality Management, Assessment and Monitoring




April 2017 - March 2018

  • Problem Statement

    To devise a digitisation (MIS) solution monitoring Water Quality Management in water supply network at district level

    ​
  • Description

    Extensive research work was done to the check on current implementations of WQM, and it was found that data collection is not up to mark and is done sporadically. An end-to-end solution to the problem was proposed, with a hardware WQM kit that gauges the water sample for parameters such as pH, EC, Temperature, Depth, and Turbidity. These parameters are measured, water quality is monitored and sent over to the cloud. Detailed analytics is done and water bodies are marked as safe or unsafe after assessing these parameters and checking if they lie in the range as specified in BIS standards. If a water body is found unsafe, immediately it is flagged unsafe in the database along with the reason of impurity and probable source of pollution.
    Successfully fabricated and improved base prototype for deployment on-site under the Department of Science and Technology, Govt. of India

    Objectives:

    1. Test water samples for pH, EC, turbidity, temperature and various other parameters based on region-specific requirement and availability of sensors.
    2. Monitor water samples at district level, state level and integrating data on a central server.
    3. Maintain records and analyse previous results to further predict usability.
    4. Create a community database of all nearby water bodies, with water availability, quality assessment information and usage statistics (accessible both offline and online).
    5. Improve the notification backend to support regional languages and adding Interactive Voice Response (IVR).
  • Tools/Technologies
    1. Data (OGD) - https://data.gov.in/dataset-group-name/water-quality
    2. Analytics - Python, Pandas, matplotlib, sqlite3, Orange
    3. Dashboard - HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, MySQL, charts.js, ngrok
    4. Database - Amazon RDS + S3 with MySQL
    5. Boards/Sensors - Raspberry Pi 3B, Arduino Mega + Turbidity, pH, EC, Temperature sensors
